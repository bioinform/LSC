Long Reads (lr)
	A long read, generally assumed to be produced by a third generation sequencer e.g. Pac Bio
	On the order of thousands of bases

Short Reads (sr)
	A short read, generally assumed to be produced by a second generation sequence e.g. Illumina
	On the order of a hundred bases

Corrections
	There are four types of corrections
	Insertion (+), deletion (-), SNP (>) and homopolymer repetition count correction (HC)
